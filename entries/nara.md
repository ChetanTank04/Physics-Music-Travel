<p>It was an out-of-the-world experience, if I just need to say one statement about visiting Nara. We stayed at Kyoto for five nights during our Japan visit. Nara was one of our side trips from there. We visited Nara in the morning and we were going to visit Osaka in the evening.</p>
<p>We left early in the morning from the Kyoto Shinkansen station. I think it was around a one-and-a-half hour journey from Kyoto, as it wasn&rsquo;t a bullet train. But the journey was pleasant and on time, as it always happens in Japan.</p>
<p>From Nara station there is a free bus to the park where all the main attractions are based. In the park everything is approachable on foot.
