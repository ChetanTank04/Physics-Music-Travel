<div class="verse ">You never know
What will you feel
When you will see her for the first time.
No,
not first time in real,
You may be known her for a while
But not like this,
It will be like you got new eyes
or she is like a whole new person to you.
It will be like you don't know her at all,
It will be like you were waiting for her.
and you will be sure that your wait is over.
Yes, It's exactly like movies
Everybody stops moving,
Time takes a pause to watch the moment
You don't hear anybody, anything
but her voice
It will be like you missed a beat,
But you will be sure,
that
this is it
this is it.</div>
