<p>Srinagar old town has its own nostalgic beauty in itself. We walked through it in the evening. We started from the zero bridge, walked through the promenade, visited a few cafes and some historical shops, buildings and landmarks. We entered the old town, 7 bridges, Hamdan E Sharif, Jamia Masjid and the vibrant local market. On every step you will find the history of hundreds of years telling the story. Also a sense of something missing, like evening blues.</p>
<p>I visited Srinagar for the second time. It is a changed city now. Kashmir has changed many times in the last millennium. Hope this time is for the good of the people living there.</p>
