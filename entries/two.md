<div class="verse ">Have you ever thought that whatever you did or earn or burn,
doesn't matter.
You walked so long, you sweated along,
You worked so hard, you have teared apart.
you left things which you not wanted to.
you kept things which not wanted you.
you left whom you loved,
you loved whom you met.
you wrote a lot, you sang a lot, you played your own part of the tune you got.
you fought, you won, you laughed, you lost, you loved, you cried,
You wanted to die when you meant to live,
you wanted to keep when you meant to give.
It is painful,
yes,
because I know you.
I know me.
and I also know that,
it doesn't matter.
It never mattered,
It never does.</div>
