<div class="verse ">You say her what do you want to say,
Don't be fearful
Don't think what she will think
What others will think.
If you think this is the person you were searching for
If you are sure she is the one who you want to grow old with.
You say her.
Say her everything what do you feel about.
Say her how beautiful she is to your eyes.
Say her every word you thought about saying when she wasn't there,
Say her your blesses and your pains.
Say her what do you feel when you say her name.
Say her what happens when you think of her.
Say her why do you wish to be with her and
Say her what if she will not be there with you.
You say her everything in a calm and soothing voice.
Don't ask any questions.
Don't expect for any answers.
Just be with her.
Look into her eyes
and say her..</div>
